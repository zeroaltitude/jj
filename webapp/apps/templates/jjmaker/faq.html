FAQ

JotJournal is designed to be a super simple way to capture your Facebook posts and photos in a great-looking keepsake, without requiring you to do any work other than clicking on a single button.

Answers to common questions are below.  Still need help?  Email us at help@jotjournal.com

Why do you ask for so much information when I connect with Facebook?

In order for us to create your book, we need access to your photos and status updates.  We use additional information as well.  For example, we need access to comments and 'likes' in order to determine which items were the most popular.   

How do you choose the content that goes into my JotJournal?

Each JotJournal has a fixed number of pages.  Working backwards from your most recent posts, we pull enough content (pictures and status updates) to fill each page of the book.  The title of your book will show you the dates included (i.e. September 15, 2010 to November 4, 2010).  Depending on how often you post to Facebook, your JotJournal will cover longer or shorter periods of time.

Can I change the period of time covered by my Journal?

At this point it is not possible to specify a different date range for your JotJournal.

Can I delete a photo or status update from my JotJournal?

Yes.  Just click on the 'x' in the upper right hand corner.  After you've marked all of the items you want to delete, hit 'remake.'  We'll pull some additional content from your stream to replace the deleted items.  Review it again, and delete again.  You can do this as many times as you wish.  Deleting something from your book does not delete it from Facebook. 

I share pictures and status updates with a Facebook group.  Will these be included in my JotJournal?

Facebook does not currently include photos and updates posted to a group as part of the news stream we obtain through our application.

Why does my JotJournal not include any status updates with links?

Part of making JotJournal easy to use is making some intelligent guesses about content that just doesn't work in a printed book.  Since you can√ït click a hyperlink that's on paper, we skip these status updates when pulling from your stream.

How quickly will I get my JotJournal?

It takes about a 10 to 14 days to receive your JotJournal from the time you place your order.
